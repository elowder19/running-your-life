The alarm system

Think about this first
Your brain thinks it can remember everything. It can't.
The Point
Your brain is not a calendar. It is great at a lot of things — but passively holding onto future obligations while you go about your day is not one of them. An alarm or reminder is not a weakness or a crutch. It is a system. People who are consistently on time and on top of things are not smarter — they just set more reminders. For each scenario below, decide: does this need an alarm, or can your brain handle it?
