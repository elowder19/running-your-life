When things go wrong

Think about this first
Have you ever reacted to something and immediately knew — even before it was over — that you just made it worse?
The Point
That is not a character flaw. That is your amygdala getting there before your prefrontal cortex had a chance to run the numbers. Your amygdala processes a threat in milliseconds — it does not wait for context, it does not calculate consequences, it just fires. Your prefrontal cortex is slower. By the time it is online, the amygdala has already launched the response. The skill is not never getting triggered. The skill is buying your prefrontal cortex enough time to catch up. Stop. Think.
