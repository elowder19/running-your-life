How to ask for stuff

Think about this first
Most people either don't ask for what they need — or ask in a way that makes it easy to say no.
The Point
There is a skill to asking for things at work. Timing, framing, and tone all affect the outcome — sometimes more than the actual request. The framework is simple: a good opener sets the tone, a clear ask makes it easy to say yes, and a strong closer shows you are thinking about their side too.
