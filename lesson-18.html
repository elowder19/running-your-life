How the government actually works

Think about this first
Most people have strong opinions about the government. Fewer people understand how it actually operates. Those two things are connected.
The Point
You cannot hold the government accountable for things you do not understand. This lesson covers three things: what most people get wrong about how government works, how a bill actually becomes law, and what the three branches are actually for. None of this is complicated — it just was not explained clearly the first time.
