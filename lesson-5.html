Most people can tell you exactly what they want. Almost nobody has thought about what it actually takes to get there.
The Point
"I want to save money" is not a goal — it is a wish. A goal has five things: it is Specific, Measurable, Achievable, Relevant, and Time-bound. That is what makes it real. Without all five, you have a direction but no destination. Run through the scenarios below — for each person, help turn their vague wish into an actual SMART goal.
