The Point
Small amounts of money feel small. That is the whole trick. $8 at the gas station does not feel like anything. Neither does $12 at the drive thru. Or $15 for a subscription. But the same math that makes spending invisible makes saving powerful. Run through each scenario below — drag the sliders to match your actual habits and watch what the numbers do over time.
Apply It
Look at your actual habits — the drive thru runs, the gas station stops, the subscriptions you forgot you had. Pick the one that surprised you the most. What would change if you redirected even half of that money into savings for one year?
