Working backwards

Think about this first
Most people start at the beginning and hope they make it to the end in time. That is not a plan — that is a wish.
The Point
Working backwards means starting at the deadline and asking: what has to happen right before this? And right before that? You build the plan from the end to the beginning — which means by the time you start, you already know exactly where you are going and how long you have. Apply It
Think of something you have coming up — a shift, an assignment, a goal. What is the deadline? Now work backwards — what has to happen the day before? The week before? What do you need to do today?
