Think about this first
Politics is not about picking a team. It is about who controls your paycheck, your rent, your healthcare, and your future. So you might want to know what they are actually doing.
The Point
Most people get their political information from people who already have an opinion about it — which means most people are getting a filtered version of reality. Every item below is verified and sourced. Some will surprise you. Some will make you angry. Some are from Republicans, some from Democrats. None of them require you to pick a side. They just require you to know what is actually going on.
